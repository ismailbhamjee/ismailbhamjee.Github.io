Interests and Hobbies

Interests and hobbies are integral part of character development. Furthermore, they are talking points in conversation and through them people can form links and converse with one another with ease. .

football

First and foremost. Sport. I am big advocate of sports in terms of playing and watching them. My favourite sport is football and the team I support is Liverpool FC. I have been a LFC fan since I can remember. For me I’ve seen the club go through highs and lows. From not winning anything for years to the Champions league and premier league victories.

Photography and travel

This is more of a passion for me as I love to visit places and the photos, I take capture my personal instances there. I usually know where I took the photos. As for travel I’ve been to a few European countries. My next aim is to go Far East or Africa. For the scenery and wildlife there would make the trip worth it.

Gaming

Gaming is one of my favourite pass times. I enjoy role playing games such as the Witcher 3 and Destiny. I also like playing multiplayer games such as call of duty and fifa with my friend whenever we’re all online.
